Admission Procedure

B.TECH

A Candidate for admission to the four-year degree course in Engineering must have passed the Intermediate examination of the Board of Intermediate Education, Government of Andhra Pradesh with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry as mandatory subjects, or any other 10+2 level examination recognized by the Board of Intermediate as equivalent thereto. The admission to the B-Tech. Course, under “Category A” seats, is through a State-Level Entrance Test called “Engineering, Agricultural Sciences and Medical Common Entrance Test” (APEAPCET), conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.
70% of the seats under “Category A” are filled by the Convener of APEAPCET based on the rank secured in APEAPCET and as per the rules of reservation in force. The Management fills 30% of the seats under “Category B”.Based on the guidelines issued by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

LINK https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/EAPCET/Eapcet/EAPCET_HomePage.aspx

College code: SRET

B.Tech (Lateral Entry)

The Convener of ECET admits candidates at 2nd year of BE (lateral entry), from the stream of Diploma Holders and B.Sc. Graduates with Mathematics as one of the subjects based on the rank secured in Engineering Common Entrance Test (ECET) to an extent of 10% of the sanctioned intake, on supernumerary basis. FEES: As applicable in case of “Category A” seats.

LINK: https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ECET/ECET/ECET_HomePage.aspx

College code: SRET

Mtech

The Convener of GATE/PGECET admits candidates at M.Tech Course, from the stream of Degree Holders based on the rank secured in Common Entrance Test (CET) to an extent as per the rules of reservation in force in 70% of “CATEGORY A” seats. The Management fills 30% of the seats under “Category B”. Based on the guidelines issued by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

LINK: https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/PGECET/PGECET/PGECET_HomePage.aspx


College code: SRET

MBA

The Convener of ICET admits candidates at MBA Course, from the stream of Degree Holders based on the rank secured in Integrated Common Entrance Test (ICET) to an extent as per the rules of reservation in force in 70% of “CATEGORY A” seats. The Management fills 30% of the seats under “Category B”.Based on the guidelines issued by the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

LINK: https://cets.apsche.ap.gov.in/ICET/ICET/ICET_HomePage.aspx

College code: SRET
